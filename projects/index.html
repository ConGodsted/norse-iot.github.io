Humidity Project

In early 2023, Griffin Hall flooded. Even after the water was cleared, the building remained too humid to work in. Club Advisor Chris Brewer and members of n-IoT decided to install humidity sensors in the building to monitor the ongoing situation. Information was visible on an easy to read dashboard containing graphs and data recieved by the sensors. The project recieved attention from the Dean of the College of Informatics, as well as NKU maintenance, and was spotlighted by the Northerner in February 2023. The dashboard was shut down after the situation was resolved in Summer 2023.

Website Workshops

In 2022, the club focused on informing NKU students about NKU's personal websites. The club hosted an event that taught students basic HTML and CSS and provided templates for students to build off and host on an NKU provided personal website.
